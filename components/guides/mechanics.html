Mechanics Reference

Trials

Main way to gain Energy. Play cards of the same element as the trial element to gain Energy.
Note: Spell Cards will not grant Energy.

Influence

(Takeover only) Win condition for Takeover Trials. Gained when using Hero Abilities or when only your opponent skips a Trial. Lost when your opponent uses Hero Abilities or when only you skip a Trial.

Seals

(Conquest only) Win condition for Makari-Hari's side in Conquest Trials.
Only gained when Makari-Hari's side uses a Hero ability (Cannot be lost by skipping trials)

Energy

Gained when playing cards in the Trial Phase or with certain Hero Abilities (See more: Card List)
(Conquest) When a player has 0 Energy remaining, they will turn to the opposite side.

Exchange

Exchange 3 Cards for 1 Energy.
(Takeover) Can only be used when under 2 Energy.
(Conquest) Can only be used when you have 0 Energy.
Note: Exchange cost can be reduced to 2 Cards by summoning Gaios (See: Card List)

Heroes

Most Heroes require Energy to summon and use.
(Takeover) Main way to gain Influence.
(Conquest) Only way to gain Seals while on Makari-Hari's side.
See more: Card List

Disparity

(Takeover only) Gains 1 charge when skipping a Trial.
When holding 3 charges, you can preform "Disparage". (Doubles all Influence gained)

Resolve

(Conquest only) Gained when Mukaki's side uses Hero Abilities, prevents players affected by Resolve from turning over to Makari-Hari's side.
Each player has a power value calculated by: (Energy x 2) + Cards.
This power value determines who gets Resolve first. (if tied, a player without Energy gets affected instead)
