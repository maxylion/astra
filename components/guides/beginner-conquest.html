Part I: Gather 'Round!

Basic Structure

You may have noticed that there a few things missing from the UI, mainly the Influence and Disparity meters. That's because the win conditions are a bit different from Takeover! First off: Makari-Hari's side's win condition stays relatively unchanged: Simply activate Hero Abilities to gain Seals; When you have 5 Seals, Makari-Hari's side wins the game.
Mukaki's side, however, has a different win condition: Turn all players on Makari-Hari's side to yours! How can you do that, though? Well, I'll explain that in the next section:

Trials, Energy & Player flipping

Unlike Takeover, skipping Trials does not cost Influence, it costs you (and whoever else skips the Trial) 1 Energy. When a Player has 0 energy, they usually flip to the other side. (we'll cover that later)
Energy can still be gained by playing cards and certain Hero Abilities.

Hero cards

Something you may have noticed is that, after finishing the first Trial Stage, your Hero Cards cost more energy to summon. (and use!) This is simply for balance purposes. As such, their costs increase with the amount of Players in the game.
You also may have noticed that Hero Cards have different effects. (See: Card Reference (Conquest) ) Again, this is simply for balance purposes.

Exchange

Exchange is almost unchanged, except for one caveat: You can only preform an Exchange if you have 0 Energy left. Keep this in mind!
