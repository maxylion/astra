Astatos v0.6 summary (As of March 4th, 2023)

Preface / Introduction

Welcome to the first Version Summary on this website! These are meant to document Astatos' changelog/update history. Without further ado:

v0.6 - Drafting

Hero Card Drafting

This replaces the Hero Card preferences in the Takeover/Conquest Trial menus.
You can Ban or Pick Hero Cards. Banned Hero Cards can't be picked/banned by anyone else.
If no Players have a certain Hero Pack, Heroes from that pack will not be selectable.

Stickers

Can be used while waiting in rooms, during multiplayer (Takeover/Conquest) and the victory screen.
Sticker Pack I is unlocked by default, with Sticker Pack II available in the Shop for 7,500 Monera

Takeover Leaderboard

Added a Leaderboard display that shows your Rank and ELO.
Each season lasts 3 months. When a season ends, players in the top 100 get a hidden title from that season.

v0.6.1 - Exchange

Exchange

Allows Players to convert Cards into Energy at a rate of 3 Cards -> 1 Energy.

Changes

+ Rewards given for Training Trials have increased from 70% to 80%.
+ Reward Points given have been increased by 25% on average per match.
+ The per match cap for Reward Points has been increased from 90 to 120 for Conquest Trials.
+ All rewards have been increased by 50% when playing a Conquest Trial with 5 or more players.
+ The EXP cap has been increased when playing a Conquest Trial with 5 or more players.

= (Takeover) Changed Makari-Hari's ability: "When Makari-Hari's side uses a Spell Card, they draw one additional Card. (Passive) (5 Cooldown) Lose one Influence, and obtain one Energy per Card held by Mukaki's side."
= (Takeover/Conuqest) Changed Gaios' ability: "The cost to perform an Exchange is reduced to two Cards for Makari-Hari/Mukaki's side. (Passive) (Once per round)"

Takeover Leaderboard

Added a Leaderboard display that shows your Rank and ELO.
Each season lasts 3 months. When a season ends, players in the top 100 get a hidden title from that season.
