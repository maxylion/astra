Part 0: Initiation

So, you want to learn how to play Astatos? If so, you've come to the right place!
In this tutorial, I, Max, will guide you through the basics of this game's PvP mode: Takeover!
As soon as you start an online game, (Through the Trial -> Takeover menu) you'll have to pick Heroes to ban (1 per player) and pick Heroes to use. (5 per player) Only then can the game really start.
After a short loading screen, you'll have to pick between two cards; These determine the side you're on, (Makari-Hari or Mukaki) But we'll get to that later.
Then, you'll be given four cards, which you can select to reroll or keep.
You'll have to wait for your opponent to finish their hand, and then... The Trials begin.

Part I: The Trial Begins

After taking part in a trial, you most likely played a card (or two!) and got energy from it. That's great! But what can you use it for?
Well, energy has a few uses, but they're all centered on one mechanic: Heroes! These cards have different effects, and are part of the win condition for Takeover: Influence!
Your influence has two indicators: The bar at the top, and the card displayed in the lower left of the screen, after you reach 5 influence points for your side, you win!
I've been going on about influence, but how do you even get it? As I (somewhat) said before, you use different heroes' abilities, of course!
