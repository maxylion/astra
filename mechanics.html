Mechanics Overview

Trials

Main way to gain Energy

Use Cards and Spell cards that match the Trial Element

Influence

Win condition for Takeover games

Gained by using Hero Abilities

Energy

Used to summon Heroes and use their Hero Abilities

Obtaining method #1: Participating in Trials

Obtaining method #2: Using certain Hero Abilities

Exchange (Takeover)

Action: Exchange 3 cards for 1 Energy

Note: Can be reduced to 2 Cards with Gaios' passive ability

Note: Can only be used when you have less than 2 Energy

See more: Card Reference

Cards

Red, Green, Blue and Yellow elements

Can be played in Trials

Heroes

Can be summoned (costs Energy)

See more: Card Reference

Disparity

(Takeover only)

Action: Disparage (costs 3 disparity)

Effect: Influence gain is doubled.

Obtaining method: Skipping Trials grants 1 disparity

Resolve

(Conquest only)

Effect: Prevents Players on Mukaki's side from flipping sides.

Obtaining method: Using Hero Abilities

Notes: Each player has a Power value that determines who becomes resolved first

Notes: Power = (Energy x 2) + Cards

Notes: If tied, a player without Energy will be picked first.
